MatchItIfYouCan!. The Easy to Learn, Exciting to play, Awesome fun Game.

Match It If You Can! is an exciting card game where you match either the color or the shape of the top card on the discard pile and claim the pile! Players with no cards get bumped, and the last player remaining wins.

Welcome to AweSome Fun Game.

This is a simple game where you can win by getting 3 of your pieces in a straight line on 7 different locations on the board. It is a variant of the traditional game Nava Kankari . Purchase a set with a hand crafted wooden board and 3D printed game pieces and get started having Awesome Fun!.
